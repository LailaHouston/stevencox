What Does Healthcare Consist of in Our Office?

In our office, your healthcare is based on your specific choices, needs, and rate of progress. Because there are many layers to peel (physical, emotional, and chemical) in the healing process and because these layers are unique to every individual....

The Problem

We are bombarded with information that is either in conflict with our uniqueness or too much for our nervous systems to process effectively. Some of this information is inherited. Without knowing which information is false for us, or having a strategy to deal with the information that we aren't processing efficiently, we begin to compensate for the conflict and excessiveness. This creates tension in the body/mind relationship and produces excesses and deficiencies among the organs and tissues of the body. When the coordinating function between our nervous system and body cells are interfered with, our body systems lose their harmonious relationships and function at a decreased capacity. As this continues, things break down an dmore dysfunction occurs. Our Ability to express the ideal self is blocked.

The Solution

To facilitate awareness and ease within the nervous system so that it can coordinate a strategy to eliminate information that fuels conflict or that has been processed inefficiently. As you see clearly with a mind that is at ease, you effectively choose to express the best you that is waiting to come out. The process of finding the ideal you and expressing it is very rewarding in that it produces a state within you that is free from conflict and allows your body to organize and heal into a beautiful order without being hindered. This order is facilitated through deep coordination within the workings of the micro-structures within each of your body cells.
