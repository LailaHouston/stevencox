Dr. Steven Cox

Dr. Cox is a native of Mississippi and received his undergraduate degree from Mississippi State University. After this he took a teaching position for a top pharmaceutical company where he focused on the central nervous system, cardiovascular medicine, and infectious disease. After seeing a questionable trend in the pharmaceutical industry, he pursued a doctoral program at Logan's University's College of Chiropractic. In addition to the traditional training, Dr. Cox completed several thousand combined hours of post-doctoral training in advance neurology, acupuncture/meridian therapies, functional medicine, applied kinesiology, and many specialized mind/ body techniques. Dr. Cox practiced in Virginia, Mississippi, and Tennessee. He has practiced for over 20 years and attracted a wide array of patients with conditions ranging from acute muscle and joint injuries to various chronic problems such as pain/inflammatory syndromes, hormone imbalances, digestive dysfunction, allergies, fatigue, and many stress related disorders.
