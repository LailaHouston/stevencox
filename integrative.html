WHAT IS CHIROPRACTIC?

The science of applied neurophysiologic diagnosis based on the theory that health and disease are life processes related to the function of the nervous system: irritation of the nervous system by mechanical, chemical, or psychic factors is the cause of disease; restoration and maintenance of health depend on normal function of the nervous. Diagnosis is the identification of these noxious irritants and treatment is their removal by the most conservative method.
