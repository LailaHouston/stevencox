Dr. Steven Cox, D.C., P.Ac.

Owner/Clinician

Dr. Cox is a native of Mississippi and received his undergraduate degree from Mississippi State University. After this he took a teaching position for a top pharmaceutical company where he focused on the central nervous system, cardiovascular medicine, and infectious disease. After seeing a questionable trend in the pharmaceutical industry, he pursued a doctoral program at Logan University's College of Chiropractic. In addition to the traditional training (which consisted of human anatomy, physiology, psychology, biochemistry, nutrition, neurology, biomechanics, physical diagnosis, lab diagnosis, philosophy, and many conservative natural health techniques), Dr. Cox also completed several thousand combined hours of post-doctoral training in advanced neurology, TCM/Acupuncture, Functional Medicine, Applied Kinesiology, and many specialized mind/ body techniques.

Dr. Cox has been in clinical practice for over 22 years and attracted a wide array of patients with conditions ranging from acute muscle and joint injuries to various chronic problems such as pain/inflammatory syndromes, hormone imbalances, digestive dysfunction, allergies, fatigue, and many stress related disorders.

He is a competitive tennis player playing both in adult tournaments and team tennis. In addition to practicing several martial arts from an early age, Dr. Cox was also trained as a cavalry scout by the U.S. Army, serving in a reconnaissance role.
