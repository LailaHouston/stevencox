Why Chiropractic?

The spine and cranium (skull) house your brain and spinal cord. The central nervous system, which is comprised of your brain and spinal cord, is responsible for keeping your entire body working optimally. This vast network of nerves and receptors is the physical path of communication between you body/mind an dyour innate intelligence. This instructional energy flow creates a vibration, or tone that can be affected by negatice patterns and disease, comprising health, happiness, and soul purpose.
